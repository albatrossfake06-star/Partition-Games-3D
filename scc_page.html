how to play scc

objective: be the last player to make a move.

corner pieces: only the rightmost piece in each row can be selected (highlighted with orange borders).

starting requirement: your selection must include either the topmost cell (first corner in sequence, with special glow) OR the bottommost cell (last corner in sequence, with special glow).

continuous rule: selected corners must form a contiguous sequence along the corner path. Corners are arranged in order from top-right to bottom-left, and you can only select consecutive corners in this sequence.

win condition: the player who makes the last move wins.
